A Veggie's Tale

Provide the information requested below for a funny story.







Story

Once upon a time there was a little boy named Billy. He was not like all the other children, because he had a very big secret. Everyday he went to school hoping no one had discovered the truth. You see Billy's parents were really Pickle. How he turned out to be a normal boy, he didn't know.

It was time for parent teacher conferences and Billy was afraid of everyone finding out his secret. How, oh how, would he explain!? Hey, wait a minute! When he looked around he saw Michelle, the most popular girl in class and her parents were Garlic.

Wow, what a relief, Billy wasn't so different after all!
